My Rig 2017

February 24, 2017

Reading time ~2 minutes

My Rig 2017

My best friend, Patrick, sent me a link to a new build with which he’s toying. He’s got his eye on a new RYZEN CPU and I can’t say that I blame him. Looking at his list, however, reminded me that I’ve been meaning to put together a list for my current rig. Not for bragging rights or anything, I hardly think there’s much to brag about, but for documentation and reference.

The two monitors that are listed at zero weren’t purchased online, so I don’t have record of what I paid for them. Also, I didn’t actually pay for Windows 10, but I did pay the listed price for Windows 7 that I upgraded to 10 for free.

As it turns out, that memory isn’t totally compatible with my CPU. I thought I had looked pretty carefully at that, but that just goes to show, building a custom PC is really difficult because the smallest details matter. Do your homework and make sure you get it right. It’s also worth noting that PCPartPicker can tell you these things and no this is not a sponsored post. Who would sponsor me anyway.
